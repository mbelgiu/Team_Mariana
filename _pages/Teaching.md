Mariana teaches and supervises students in Earth Observation, optical remote sensing, image analysis, machine learning, deep learning, and geospatial data science at the Faculty ITC, University of Twente.

<section class="teaching-overview" markdown="1">

## Teaching areas

- Optical remote sensing and image analysis.
- Machine learning and deep learning for geospatial sciences.
- Space-time thinking for spatial and Earth Observation data.
- Internships and MSc thesis supervision on crop monitoring, environmental mapping, food security, and urban vulnerability.
