### Dr. Mariana Belgiu

Mariana Belgiu is an Associate Professor in GeoAI and multi-temporal remote sensing for agri-environmental studies in the Department of Earth Observation Science at the Faculty ITC, University of Twente. She holds a PhD in Remote Sensing and an MSc in Applied Geoinformatics from the University of Salzburg.

Her research develops AI and multi-temporal remote sensing methods for environmental challenges, with current emphasis on data-centric AI for Earth Observation, crop mapping and monitoring, yield forecasting, hidden hunger and crop nutrition, scarce-label learning, transferability, urban vulnerability, and inclusive Earth Observation science.
