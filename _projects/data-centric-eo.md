This research direction develops GeoAI and data-centric AI methods for multi-temporal Earth Observation data. The work focuses on extracting information from optical, hyperspectral, UAV, and radar imagery while addressing scarce labels, data quality, and model transferability across regions and sensing conditions.

Applications include land cover mapping, cultivated land monitoring, crop production analysis, yield forecasting, soil and crop nutrient estimation, and environmental monitoring at multiple geographic scales.
