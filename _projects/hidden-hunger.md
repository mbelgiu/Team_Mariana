This line of work explores how Earth Observation and spatial data can help assess hidden hunger, a food security challenge linked to micronutrient deficiencies. Projects such as EO4Nutri and HyNutri bring together satellite, UAV, and hyperspectral data to estimate and predict nutrient levels in soil, crop canopy, and harvested grains.

The aim is to create scalable methods that complement laboratory analysis and help connect crop quality, agricultural management, and human nutrition. The work also draws on crop quality mapping, yield forecasting, and spatial data integration to support food security research.
