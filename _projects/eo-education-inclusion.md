This work connects Earth Observation research with education, skills, and inclusion. It includes teaching in image analysis and machine learning at ITC, work on space-oriented curricula and skills gaps through ASTRAIOS and EO4GEO, and participation in EO4all, a working group focused on inclusive Earth Observation science and gender equity.

The emphasis is on making Earth Observation methods more visible, usable, and accessible to diverse research and policy communities, from MSc training to international capacity-building networks such as EENSAT.
